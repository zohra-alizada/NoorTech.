Mission of NoorTech

NoorTech will help Afghanistan by expanding access to education and digital skills in local languages. It empowers women, youth, and rural communities through solar-powered, offline technology. By enabling remote work and small businesses, it boosts income and self-reliance. Overall, NoorTech supports a more connected, educated, and innovative Afghanistan.
